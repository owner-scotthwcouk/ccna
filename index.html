OSI & TCP/IP Models

Domain 1

Map how data moves from application to wire. Focus on encapsulation, PDUs, and which devices operate at each layer.

  • Explain each layer with a real protocol example.
  • Compare TCP/IP vs OSI layers side-by-side.
  • Identify where switches vs routers make decisions.

Deep Dive

Build a cheat sheet mapping HTTP, DNS, ARP, ICMP, and Ethernet to their layers. Practice explaining a full packet journey.

Subnetting & IPv4 Design

Domain 1

Master binary math, subnet masks, VLSM, and summarization. This is a high-impact exam topic.

  • Calculate subnets, hosts, and wildcards quickly.
  • Design addressing for multiple VLANs and sites.
  • Summarize routes to reduce table size.

Practice

Create 4 subnets out of a /24 and assign them to departments. Use show ip interface to validate.

Lab Ideas

VLANs & Trunking

Domain 2

Segment networks with VLANs and carry them across trunks. Understand tagging, native VLANs, and inter-VLAN routing.

  • Create VLANs and assign access ports.

Spanning Tree Protocol

Domain 2

Prevent Layer 2 loops by mastering STP/RSTP, root bridge election, and port roles.

  • Compare STP vs RSTP convergence.
  • Configure portfast and BPDU guard.

Troubleshooting

Use show spanning-tree to verify root and check for blocked ports.
